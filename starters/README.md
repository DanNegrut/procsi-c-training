# Starters

Tiered starter material handed to teams, following the camp's starter policy: nothing on
the low-floor days (days 2 to 5 start from a blank slate), and a skeleton / helper / demo
on the high-ceiling days (days 6 to 8). Day 9 needs no starter file; chrono-rag is the
starter.

| Day | Folder | Tier | What it is |
|-----|--------|------|------------|
| 6 | `day-06-euler/` | skeleton | Euler loop with the physics lines left as TODOs |
| 7 | `day-07-blender/` | helper | Blender script that keyframes the brick from the day-6 CSV, plus a sample CSV |
| 8 | `day-08-pychrono/` | demo | Complete, runnable PyChrono brick-with-friction script, plus pointers to the official demos |

Hand each folder out the morning of its day, not before.
